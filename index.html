Ahmednagar: The Historic City of Maharashtra


Ahmednagar is a historic city in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is known for its rich history, military presence, and significant contributions to India's independence movement. Here are some key details about Ahmednagar:

General Information:

  • Location: Western Maharashtra, India
  • Founded by: Ahmad Nizam Shah in 1490
  • District: Ahmednagar
  • Official Language: Marathi
  • Population: Around 350,000 (city), over 4.5 million (district)
  • Famous for: Historical forts, religious sites, sugar industry, and military establishments
